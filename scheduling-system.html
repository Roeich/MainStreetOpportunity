How It Works

1

Lead Engagement

Prospects engage with your marketing materials (website, landing page, email, or ad) and indicate interest in your services.

2

Scheduling Widget Activation

Our system presents them with an intelligent booking interface that displays your available time slots based on your preferences and current calendar.

3

Instant Confirmation

The lead selects a convenient time, enters their information, and instantly receives confirmation with meeting details.

4

Automatic Calendar Update

Your calendar is automatically updated with the appointment details, including lead information and context from their marketing interaction.

5

Smart Reminder Sequence

Automated reminders are sent to both you and the lead at strategic intervals to reduce no-shows and increase preparation.
