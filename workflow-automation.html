Workflow Automation

Trigger customized workflows based on marketing campaign responses.

73% of top-performing agents cite workflow automation as critical to their success.

What is Workflow Automation?

Workflow automation intelligently manages your real estate processes by creating customized response sequences triggered by specific actions in your marketing campaigns.

Our system monitors all marketing channels and automatically initiates the right sequence of follow-up actions based on how prospects interact with your campaigns.

Key Benefits

  • Eliminates manual follow-up tasks
  • Ensures consistent client experience
  • Personalizes interactions at scale
  • Works 24/7 without your involvement

The Problem We Solve

Inconsistent Follow-Up

Most leads fall through the cracks due to manual follow-up processes that are easy to forget.

Time-Consuming Tasks

Realtors spend up to 15 hours weekly on repetitive follow-up tasks that could be automated.

Generic Communication

Manual follow-up often lacks personalization that reflects specific prospect interests.

Lost Opportunities

Without automation, response times lag, missing critical windows for engagement.

How Our Workflow Automation Works

Trigger Event

Prospect interacts with your marketing (opens email, clicks ad, submits form)

Response Analysis

System analyzes interaction type and prospect data to determine best workflow

Workflow Selection

Customized workflow activates with personalized timing and content sequences

Multi-Channel Execution

Automated actions execute across email, SMS, calls, and social media
